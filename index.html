01 — The Shop

Proper barbering, made on Bridge Street.

A traditional barbershop in the heart of Rothwell. Gents cuts, cut-throat shaves, beard trims — and the kind of unhurried, hands-on work you only get from a shop that's been doing it the right way.

NN14 · 6JW The Chair

"A proper haircut isn't complicated — sharp scissors, a steady hand, and the time to do it right. No gimmicks."

The Barbers Shop is a traditional gents' barbershop on Bridge Street, Rothwell. Gents cuts, cut-throat shaves with the lather and the steel, beard work, colouring and perms when you want them, and OAP rates for our older regulars.

Walk-ins always welcome — no appointment needed, no booking apps, no waiting list. Stroll up, take a seat, get a proper cut. Late opening Thursdays for anyone who can't make it in the day.
